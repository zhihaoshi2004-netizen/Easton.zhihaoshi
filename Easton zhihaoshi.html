Introduction to Thomas Cavendish 托马斯·卡文迪许简介

Thomas Cavendish (c. 1560 – 1592) was one of the most significant English explorers of the Elizabethan era, renowned as the second Englishman to complete a circumnavigation of the globe. His historic voyage from 1586 to 1588 expanded European knowledge of global geography, demonstrating the feasibility of long-distance maritime travel and contributing valuable data to cartography.

托马斯·卡文迪许（约1560-1592年）是伊丽莎白时代英国最重要的探险家之一，被誉为第二位完成环球航行的英国人。他在1586年至1588年的历史性航行拓展了欧洲人对全球地理的认识，证明了长途海上旅行的可行性，并为制图学提供了宝贵的数据。

Cavendish's expedition combined exploration with privateering, making him both a geographical pioneer and a symbol of England's emerging maritime power during the Age of Discovery. His journey provided critical information that helped refine 16th-century world maps and laid groundwork for future global exploration.

卡文迪许的探险结合了探索与私掠活动，使他既是地理先驱，也是大航海时代英国新兴海上力量的象征。他的旅程提供了关键信息，帮助改进了16世纪的世界地图，并为未来的全球探索奠定了基础。

"The exploration of the globe is the greatest adventure of humankind, and each voyage writes a new chapter in our understanding of the world."

"对地球的探索是人类最伟大的冒险，每一次航行都为我们对世界的理解谱写新的篇章。"

The Explorer's Life

探险家的一生

Born into a gentry family in Suffolk, England, Cavendish inherited significant wealth but sought greater fortune through overseas exploration.

卡文迪许出生于英格兰萨福克郡的一个贵族家庭，继承了可观的财富，但通过海外探险寻求更大的财富。

Following in the footsteps of Sir Francis Drake, Cavendish became the second Englishman to circumnavigate the globe between 1586 and 1588, completing his journey in 2 years and 50 days.

追随弗朗西斯·德雷克爵士的脚步，卡文迪许在1586年至1588年间成为第二位完成环球航行的英国人，用2年50天完成了他的旅程。

His second attempt at circumnavigation in 1591 ended with his disappearance at sea, likely dying from illness or storms near South America.

他在1591年第二次尝试环球航行时在海上失踪，可能是在南美洲附近死于疾病或风暴。

The "Desire"

"欲望号"

Cavendish's flagship, a 120-ton galleon that served as his primary vessel throughout the circumnavigation. It was on this ship that he returned to England in 1588, loaded with treasure from captured Spanish ships.

卡文迪许的旗舰，一艘120吨的大型帆船，是他整个环球航行中的主要船只。1588年，他就是乘坐这艘船返回英国，船上装载着从捕获的西班牙船只上获得的宝藏。

The "Hugh Gallant"

"休·格兰特号"

A smaller 40-ton vessel that accompanied the expedition. It was commanded by John Davis, who later became a notable explorer in his own right. The ship was lost during the voyage.

一艘较小的40吨船只，伴随探险队同行。它由约翰·戴维斯指挥，约翰后来成为了一位著名的探险家。这艘船在航行中失踪。

The "Content"

"满足号"

The smallest of the three ships at 30 tons. It was commanded by Thomas Fuller and was also lost during the expedition, presumably separated from the other vessels in rough seas.

三艘船中最小的一艘，重30吨。它由托马斯·富勒指挥，在探险过程中也失踪了，大概是在波涛汹涌的海面上与其他船只失散。

Historical Context

历史背景

Cavendish's voyage occurred during a period of intense rivalry between European powers for global dominance. England, seeking to challenge Spanish and Portuguese supremacy, actively supported explorers and privateers who could expand English influence and wealth.

卡文迪许的航行发生在欧洲列强为争夺全球主导地位而激烈竞争的时期。英国为了挑战西班牙和葡萄牙的霸权，积极支持能够扩大英国影响力和财富的探险家和私掠者。

Geographic Inquiries

地理探索问题

Maps of this period showed Europe, Asia, Africa, and the Americas, along with speculative "Terra Australis Incognita" (unknown southern land). These maps were often inaccurate by modern standards but represented the best geographical knowledge of the time.

这一时期的地图显示了欧洲、亚洲、非洲和美洲，以及推测的"未知南方大陆"。按照现代标准，这些地图往往不准确，但代表了当时最好的地理知识。

His detailed observations corrected map inaccuracies, particularly regarding Pacific coastlines, currents, and distances between continents. Cavendish's logs recorded latitude measurements and coastal features that helped cartographers create more accurate representations of the world.

他的详细观察纠正了地图的不准确之处，特别是关于太平洋海岸线、洋流和大陆之间的距离。卡文迪许的日志记录了纬度测量和海岸特征，帮助制图师创建更准确的世界地图。

Lines like the equator and Tropic of Cancer helped navigators determine their position, critical for oceanic voyages before modern technology. By measuring the angle of celestial bodies, explorers could calculate their latitude and stay on course across vast oceans.

像赤道和北回归线这样的线条帮助航海者确定自己的位置，这在现代技术出现之前对远洋航行至关重要。通过测量天体的角度，探险家可以计算出自己的纬度，并在广阔的海洋上保持航向。

It confirmed the Earth's spherical shape and demonstrated the interconnectedness of global regions, laying groundwork for global trade. These voyages showed that the world was larger and more diverse than previously imagined, sparking further exploration and colonization.

它证实了地球是球形的，并展示了全球各地区的相互联系，为全球贸易奠定了基础。这些航行表明，世界比以前想象的更大、更多样化，引发了进一步的探索和殖民。

July 1586

Voyage Begins

航行开始

Cavendish departs Plymouth, England with three ships: the "Desire" (his flagship), the "Hugh Gallant," and the "Content." His crew numbers approximately 123 men. The expedition combines exploration with privateering against Spanish interests.

卡文迪许带着三艘船从英格兰普利茅斯出发："欲望号"（他的旗舰）、"休·格兰特号"和"满足号"。他的船员约有123人。这次探险结合了探索和针对西班牙利益的私掠活动。

November 1586

Strait of Magellan

麦哲伦海峡

Cavendish successfully navigates the Strait of Magellan, a treacherous waterway connecting the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans. This difficult passage takes over a month to traverse, with dangerous currents and unpredictable weather.

卡文迪许成功穿越了连接大西洋和太平洋的危险水道——麦哲伦海峡。这段艰难的航道需要一个多月才能通过，有危险的洋流和不可预测的天气。

February 1587

Capture of the "Santa Ana"

捕获"圣安娜号"

Cavendish captures the Spanish galleon "Santa Ana" off the coast of Mexico, seizing a valuable cargo including 122,000 pesos of gold and silver, silk, and other goods. This capture alone makes the expedition highly profitable.

卡文迪许在墨西哥海岸捕获了西班牙大帆船"圣安娜号"，缴获了包括122,000比索的金银、丝绸和其他货物在内的贵重货物。仅此一役，这次探险就获得了高额利润。

May 1587

Pacific Crossing

穿越太平洋

Cavendish begins his crossing of the Pacific Ocean, heading westward. The voyage across the world's largest ocean takes approximately three months, with the crew surviving on limited provisions.

卡文迪许开始向西穿越太平洋。穿越这个世界上最大的海洋的航行大约需要三个月，船员们依靠有限的给养生存。

September 1588

Return to England

返回英国

After rounding the Cape of Good Hope, Cavendish returns to Plymouth, completing his circumnavigation in 2 years and 50 days. Only one ship, the "Desire," completes the journey. Cavendish becomes a national hero and one of England's richest men.

绕过好望角后，卡文迪许返回普利茅斯，用2年50天完成了他的环球航行。只有一艘船——"欲望号"完成了这段旅程。卡文迪许成为民族英雄和英国最富有的人之一。

Historical Legacy 历史遗产

Exploration Impact

探索影响

Cavendish's successful circumnavigation demonstrated the practicality of global travel, inspiring subsequent explorers and merchants.

卡文迪许成功的环球航行证明了全球旅行的可行性，激励了后来的探险家和商人。

His detailed logs provided valuable data for improving nautical charts, making oceanic navigation safer and more reliable for future generations.

他的详细日志为改进海图提供了宝贵的数据，使未来几代人的海洋航行更加安全可靠。

The voyage helped establish England as a serious maritime competitor to Spain and Portugal during the Age of Exploration.

这次航行帮助英国在大航海时代成为西班牙和葡萄牙的重要海上竞争对手。

Historical Significance

历史意义

Cavendish's expedition contributed to the scientific revolution by providing empirical evidence that supported new geographical theories.

卡文迪许的探险通过提供支持新地理理论的经验证据，为科学革命做出了贡献。

His observations of different cultures, flora, and fauna during his journey added to Europe's understanding of global diversity.

他在旅途中对不同文化、动植物的观察，增加了欧洲对全球多样性的理解。

As one of the few Elizabethan circumnavigators, Cavendish helped pave the way for Britain's eventual global maritime empire.

作为为数不多的伊丽莎白时代环球航行者之一，卡文迪许为英国最终建立全球海上帝国铺平了道路。
